What We Do ?


Tech Valley Pakistan is a social enterprise that provides solutions, consultation and services to leading organizations, institutes, governments and communities to enhance their existing processes and experiences along with strengthening their current capabilities.


Tech Valley Pakistan being the official trusted country partner of Google also provides cloud-based services making workplaces and educational institutions smart and technologically equipped. From deployment to training, we will help with the businesses to successfully migrate their infrastructure to the cloud whilst introducing modern Google’s Machine Learning and Data techniques providing unlimited productivity and development to your work.

Our Verticals

Tech Valley Pakistan being the official trusted country partner of Google provides

Ed Tech

Tech Valley aims to provide solutions to schools, colleges, and universities to enrich their learning and teaching experiences. We also help students learn and use technology responsibly at an early age.

Capacity Building

At Tech Valley, we ensure everyone has access to the latest knowledge and skills in the digital world. Our skill-building activities and programmes all over Pakistan.

Technology

Our technology consulting services help you steer your transformation with the latest technology, design thinking, and agility, Guiding you through your digital transformation we will bring all elements.

Entrepreneurship

Tech Valley was founded with a mission to inculcate the startup culture. We believe entrepreneurship coupled with technology has immense potential to benefit the economy.

Goverment & Public Sector

At Tech Valley, we engage all the stakeholders of a project or business through digitization and E-Governance, to ensure sustainable growth. Tech Valley has successfully connected companies and projects.

Tourism

Tech Valley is proud to have played a vital role in supporting tourism across Pakistan. We organize and actively participate in meetings with the representatives of tourism in Pakistan.
